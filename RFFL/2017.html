The Season Has Started!

So here we are, ladies and gentlemen. Another year on the statistical grid iron.

The draft complete. The lineups set. And now we get to hope, pray, and sacrifice lambs all in the name of our studs going off.

I have taken it upon myself, for another season, to provide flavor text for what might otherwise be dry week-to-week. In amongst moving players and reading gut-wrenching news, I find it fun to see how we all stack up subjectively.

My plan is to get the rankings up on this site every Wednesday evening. I might try to post interesting stats that I collect along the way. And if you’d like to suggest a graph or table that appears, I’m open to them.

Even though the rankings are subjective, and all in fun, I know some nerds like myself enjoy a good spreadsheet. The link to view such will be readily available, when the season starts, to view. It’s a fine-looking specimen, if you ask me.

And as always, good luck and have this season.
