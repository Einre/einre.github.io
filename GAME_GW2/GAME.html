[GAME] is simply a group of fun helpful people who've come together to create the community we want. We're an active Pv'everything guild who dabbles in all of Tyria's splendor from Open World to sPvP to WvW, to Fractals, Dungeons, Story Missions, Raids for fun; you name it and if we aren't doing it, we'd love to tag along with you and learn it. We have scheduled events, runs, and groups going daily but we always have time to help a Guildie out. You have a question? We'll do our best to find an answer. We are a low stress guild. You need help with this or that ask in guild chat and you won't have to go at it alone. We strive to be that person, that group you wish you'd found when you first started on your Tyrian journey. All we ask of our members is they strive to be that person too. We're all about the paying it forward.
